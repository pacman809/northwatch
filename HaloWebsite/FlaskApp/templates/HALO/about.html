"WE STRIVE TO BUILD AN "EXPLORER" THAT OFFERS COMPLETE TRANSPARENCY"

As a community, We've come together to develop what we believe is the most neccessary tool in the cryptosphere. A robust, frictionless, transparent and easy to use explorer that everyone can enjoy and explore.

Being community developed, We believe we have found the balance necessary to give the "tech savvy" and "not so tech savvy", Something easy to navigate.

We are continuously committed to keeping up with new tech and features, updating our product as needed. We hope users enjoy and utilize this explorer for as long as the cryptosphere continues to grow.
